## For Patients

### How to Book an Appointment

#### Step 1: Find Your Doctor
1. Visit the TeleCareZone main website
2. Browse through "Our Experts" section
3. Filter by speciality, experience, or fees
4. Click on "View Profile" to see doctor's full profile

#### Step 2: Visit Doctor's Page
- Each doctor has a personalized landing page (e.g., `drjohn.telecarezone.com`)
- View their:
  - Qualifications (MBBS, MD, DM, etc.)
  - Experience years
  - Area of expertise
  - Patient testimonials
  - Social media profiles
  - Consultation fees

#### Step 3: Book Appointment
1. Click "Book Appointment" button
2. **Select Time Slot:**
   - Choose from available slots (7 days in advance)
   - Slots are 9 AM to 6 PM daily
   - Select date and time convenient for you

3. **Enter Personal Information:**
   - First Name & Last Name *
   - Phone Number (with country code) *
   - Email Address *
   - Gender *
   - Age *
   - How did you hear about us?
   - Describe your health concern in detail *

4. Click "Proceed to Payment"

#### Step 4: Make Payment
- **Payment Method:** Razorpay (Credit/Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking)
- **Consultation Fee:** As mentioned on doctor's profile
- **Security:** All payments are secure and encrypted
- Click "Pay Now" to complete

#### Step 5: Confirmation
After successful payment, you will receive:

1. **Instant Confirmation:**
   - On-screen confirmation with booking ID
   - Google Meet link for video consultation

2. **WhatsApp Message:**
   - Appointment confirmation
   - Doctor details
   - Meeting link
   - Date and time

3. **Email:**
   - Appointment details
   - Doctor information
   - Meeting instructions

4. **Reminder:**
   - WhatsApp reminder 15 minutes before appointment

#### Step 6: Join Consultation
1. Click the Google Meet link (received via WhatsApp/Email)
2. Join 2-3 minutes before scheduled time
3. Ensure:
   - Stable internet connection
   - Working camera and microphone
   - Quiet environment
   - Medical history/reports ready

## FAQs

### For Patients

**Q: Is video consultation as good as in-person?**  
A: For many conditions, yes. Doctors can diagnose, prescribe, and provide treatment plans via video.

**Q: Is my payment secure?**  
A: Yes, all payments processed via Razorpay with bank-level encryption.

**Q: Can I cancel my appointment?**  
A: Yes, cancellation available with refund based on timing (see refund policy).

**Q: What if I miss my appointment?**  
A: No refund for no-shows. Please reschedule in advance.

### For Healthcare Professionals

**Q: How much do I earn?**  
A: You receive 90% of consultation fee, platform retains 10%.

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: Automatic settlement T+2 days to your bank account.

**Q: Can I set my own fees?**  
A: Yes, you decide your consultation fees.

**Q: Can I update my profile?**  
A: Yes, contact admin team for profile updates.

### For Administrators

**Q: How to handle disputes?**  
A: Review appointment details, check recordings (if available), mediate fairly.

**Q: What if a doctor doesn't join appointment?**  
A: Issue full refund, warn doctor, track performance.

**Q: How to verify doctor credentials?**  
A: Check medical council registration, verify certificates, confirm identity.
